About the Project

This project includes several visualizations of various categories of POIs in London, including bus stations, sport stadiums, and more. The interactive maps help understand the spatial distribution of these POIs.

Map of the Polygons of Interest (POIs)

We have six different types of POIs:

  1. the biggest bus stops,
  2. the biggest subway stations,
  3. soccer stadiums with over 25,000 people,
  4. big event places with a capacity of over 7,500,
  5. the most popular sightseeings, and
  6. royal parks
